Bayfront Hotel Cebu
Cebu

Overview: Bayfront is an affordable iconic mid-rise hotel along Kaohsiung St, Cebu city. This hotel has welcoming accommodating rooms: Standard, Superior, Suite, and “Barkada”. That is why, those who wish to get great value for their money prefer this. Moreover, Bayfront Hotel Cebu offers good deal for those holding meetings and conferences. With meals, accommodation and snacks, this hotel could already serve you those for as low as P2, 000 per day. To add, Bayfront Hotel, given its price, has one of the best locations among the hotels in Cebu. It situates across SM City mall and is near the port. The Hotel is near to Cebu’s best tourist spots. You may also check this Cebu City tour package for your hassle-free trip.
